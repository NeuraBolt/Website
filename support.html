Frequently Asked Questions

How do I unlock Pro across all my extensions?

Buy NeuraBolt Pro once in any extension. You'll get a portable NB-XXXX-XXXX code. Open the status pill in any other NeuraBolt extension, enter that code, and Pro unlocks there too.

Where do I find my Pro code?

In the extension you purchased from, click the "PRO" pill at the top — your code is shown there, with a Copy button.

How do I cancel or manage my subscription?

Billing is handled by Stripe. Use the management link from your purchase receipt, or email us and we'll help. Cancelling stops future renewals; Pro stays active until the end of your current period.

How do I request a refund?

Email info@neurabolt.ai with your purchase details and we'll do our best to help, subject to Stripe's processing.

Is my data private?

Yes — most processing happens entirely on your device. See our Privacy Policy for exactly what each extension does and doesn't send.

How do I report a bug or request a feature?

Email us with the extension name, your browser version, and what happened. Screenshots help a lot.
