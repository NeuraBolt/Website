Privacy Policy for NeuraSave

Last Updated: September 2025

Overview

NeuraSave ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our browser extension.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Email Address: Only collected if you choose to activate Pro features, used solely for subscription verification
  • Settings Preferences: Your chosen file formats, scaling options, and extension settings stored locally in your browser

Automatically Collected Information

  • Download Count: Number of images downloaded (stored locally, reset daily)
  • Extension State: Whether the extension is enabled or disabled
  • Pro Status: Whether you have an active Pro subscription

How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Verify Pro subscription status when you provide your email
  • Save your preferences for a better user experience
  • Track daily download limits (stored locally only)
  • Provide and improve extension functionality

Data Storage

  • Local Storage: All settings and preferences are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API
  • Sync Storage: Pro activation status may be synced across your devices if you're signed into Chrome/Edge
  • No Server Storage: We do not store or track your browsing history, downloaded images, or personal data on our servers

Information We DON'T Collect

  • Browsing history or activity
  • Content of images you download
  • Personal information beyond email (for Pro users only)
  • Analytics or tracking data
  • Cookies or similar tracking technologies

Third-Party Services

Stripe

  • Payment processing for Pro subscriptions is handled by Stripe
  • We never see or store your payment information
  • Stripe's privacy policy: https://stripe.com/privacy

API Services

  • Pro subscription verification uses our secure API hosted on Vercel
  • Only your email is sent to verify subscription status
  • No other personal data is transmitted

Data Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • Pro verification uses secure HTTPS connections
  • We implement industry-standard security measures
  • No sensitive data is transmitted or stored on external servers

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete all local data by uninstalling the extension
  • Opt-out of Pro features without affecting basic functionality
  • Request information about data associated with your email (Pro users)
  • Cancel your subscription at any time

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

Email: info@neurabolt.ai
Website: https://neurabolt.ai

Compliance

This extension complies with:

  • Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) where applicable

Data Deletion

To delete all data associated with NeuraSave:

  1. Uninstall the extension from your browser
  2. This will automatically remove all locally stored data
  3. Pro users can contact support to remove email from our verification system

By using NeuraSave, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

NeuraTabs Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 2025

Your Privacy is Our Priority

NeuraTabs processes all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal information or browsing data to any external servers.

What Information We Process

Locally Processed Data (Never Leaves Your Device)

  • Tab Information: Tab titles, URLs, and favicons are processed locally to organize your tabs into groups
  • Group Preferences: Your custom group names and organization preferences
  • Snooze Settings: Information about tabs you've snoozed for later

Optional Features

  • OpenAI Integration (Optional): If you choose to use AI features, you provide your own OpenAI API key. When enabled, only tab titles and domains are sent to OpenAI's servers using your personal API key. We never see or store your API key.
  • Community Mappings: Anonymous domain-to-category mappings (e.g., "spotify.com" → "Music") may be shared with our community database to improve categorization for all users. This data contains no personal information.

What We DON'T Do

  • ❌ We do NOT collect your browsing history
  • ❌ We do NOT track your personal information
  • ❌ We do NOT sell any data to third parties
  • ❌ We do NOT use your data for advertising
  • ❌ We do NOT use your data for creditworthiness or lending purposes
  • ❌ We do NOT have access to your OpenAI API key or AI queries

Data Storage

Local Storage (On Your Device)

All your personal settings, preferences, and tab organization data are stored locally using Chrome's storage API. This data:

  • Remains on your device
  • Syncs across your devices only if you're signed into Chrome with sync enabled
  • Is never accessible to us or any third party

Community Database (Anonymous)

Our optional community features use a Supabase database that stores only:

  • Domain names and their categories (e.g., "github.com" → "Dev")
  • Vote counts for categorization accuracy
  • No user identifiers or personal information

Permissions We Request

NeuraTabs requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • tabs: To read tab information for organization
  • tabGroups: To create and manage tab groups
  • storage: To save your preferences locally
  • alarms: To reopen snoozed tabs at scheduled times
  • contextMenus: To add right-click menu options
  • host_permissions: To extract favicons and process tab content

NeuraBolt Pro

If you subscribe to NeuraBolt Pro:

  • Payment is processed securely through Stripe
  • We only store your email for subscription management
  • Your payment information is never stored by us
  • You can cancel anytime through Stripe's customer portal

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there's nothing for us to delete or provide. However, you always have the right to:

  • Uninstall the extension at any time
  • Clear your local extension data through Chrome settings
  • Disable specific features like AI integration
  • Use the extension in local-only mode

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.

Open Source Commitment

We believe in transparency. NeuraTabs' categorization logic and privacy practices are open for review.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or NeuraTabs' privacy practices, please contact us at:

Email: info@neurabolt.ai
Website: neurabolt.ai
