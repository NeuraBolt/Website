Privacy Policy for NeuraPulse

Last Updated: June 2026

Overview

NeuraPulse delivers AI-generated weekly industry briefings tailored to a job role you choose. This policy explains exactly what it does and doesn't do with your data.

What We Process

  • The role you choose (e.g. "Music Producer") and any thumbs up/down feedback you give — sent to our server to generate and refine your briefing.
  • An anonymous device ID — a random string (no name, email, or device fingerprinting) used to remember your preferences and Pro status.
  • Your settings and cached briefings — stored locally in your browser.

What We DON'T Collect

  • We do NOT read the pages you visit or your browsing history (NeuraPulse has no access to web pages).
  • We do NOT collect your name, email, or location.
  • We do NOT sell or share data, use it for advertising, or for creditworthiness/lending.

How Briefings Are Generated

Briefings are generated on our server using Anthropic's Claude. Only the role and topic are included in the request — never personal data. All processing happens through our secure proxy over HTTPS.

NeuraBolt Pro & Payments

Pro is unlocked with an anonymous device ID or a portable NB-XXXX-XXXX code. Payments are handled entirely by Stripe (Stripe's privacy policy); the extension never sees your card or billing details.

Feedback

If you choose to use the in-extension Send feedback form, the message you type is sent to our server so we can read it and improve the product. An email address is sent only if you choose to add one (so we can reply). This is entirely optional and is only sent when you click Send. We do not collect any feedback automatically.

Your Rights & Data Deletion

Uninstalling the extension removes all locally stored data. Since we don't hold personal identifiers, there is no account to delete. For any request, contact info@neurabolt.ai.

Compliance

NeuraPulse complies with the Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies, GDPR, and CCPA where applicable.

NeuraTabs Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Your Privacy is Our Priority

NeuraTabs organizes your tabs on your device. Your tab titles, full URLs, and browsing history never leave your computer. The only thing that can leave your device is anonymous and one-way hashed: when you deliberately correct a site's category, a hash of the domain (not the domain itself) is shared to improve grouping for everyone — and you can turn that off.

What Information We Process

Locally Processed Data (Never Leaves Your Device)

  • Tab Information: Tab titles, URLs, and favicons are processed locally to organize your tabs into groups
  • Group Preferences: Your custom group names and organization preferences
  • Snooze Settings: Information about tabs you've snoozed for later

Shared Category Learning (Anonymous & Hashed)

When you deliberately correct a site's category, NeuraTabs can contribute that to a shared "category brain" so grouping improves for everyone. This is done in a privacy-preserving way:

  • Only one-way hashes are sent — a SHA-256 hash of the domain (never the domain itself) and the category you chose. Our server never holds a readable list of the sites you use.
  • Unlinkable votes — instead of a device ID, each vote carries a per-site token (a hash of your random device id combined with that domain). This counts one vote per device per site while making your corrections impossible to link across different sites or back to you.
  • Only deliberate corrections — the sites you simply visit are never sent. A site only becomes a shared suggestion after at least 3 devices agree (60%+).
  • Opt-out anytime — turn off contributing in Settings; removing a learned site or tapping Undo also retracts your vote.

What We DON'T Do

  • ❌ We do NOT collect your browsing history
  • ❌ We do NOT track your personal information
  • ❌ We do NOT sell any data to third parties
  • ❌ We do NOT use your data for advertising
  • ❌ We do NOT use your data for creditworthiness or lending purposes
  • ❌ We do NOT use any AI service for grouping — categorization is 100% on-device

Feedback

If you choose to use the in-extension Send feedback form, the message you type is sent to our server so we can read it and improve the product. An email address is sent only if you choose to add one (so we can reply). This is entirely optional and is only sent when you click Send. We do not collect any feedback automatically.

Data Storage

Local Storage (On Your Device)

All your personal settings, preferences, and tab organization data are stored locally using Chrome's storage API. This data:

  • Remains on your device
  • Syncs across your devices only if you're signed into Chrome with sync enabled
  • Is never accessible to us or any third party

Shared Category Database (Anonymous & Hashed)

The shared category brain stores only:

  • One-way hashes of domains and their agreed category (no plaintext domains)
  • An unlinkable per-site vote token (no device identifier)
  • Vote counts for consensus accuracy

Permissions We Request

NeuraTabs requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • tabs: To read tab titles/URLs and organize them into groups
  • tabGroups: To create and manage native tab groups
  • storage: To save your preferences and learned categories locally
  • alarms: To reopen snoozed tabs at scheduled times
  • contextMenus: To add right-click menu options

NeuraTabs requests no host permissions and never injects code into the pages you visit.

NeuraBolt Pro

  • Pro is unlocked with an anonymous device ID or a portable NB-XXXX-XXXX code.
  • Payment is processed securely by Stripe; we never store your payment details.
  • The extension does not collect your email. You can cancel anytime via Stripe.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there's nothing for us to delete or provide. However, you always have the right to:

  • Uninstall the extension at any time
  • Clear your local extension data through Chrome settings
  • Disable specific features like AI integration
  • Use the extension in local-only mode

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.

Open Source Commitment

We believe in transparency. NeuraTabs' categorization logic and privacy practices are open for review.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or NeuraTabs' privacy practices, please contact us at:

Email: info@neurabolt.ai
Website: neurabolt.ai

Privacy Policy for NeuraShot

Last Updated: June 2026

Overview

NeuraShot captures screenshots and records your screen. Everything you capture is processed and saved locally — nothing you capture is ever uploaded.

What Stays on Your Device

  • All captures & recordings: visible-area, full-page, region, and all-tabs screenshots, PDF exports, and screen recordings are created in your browser and saved straight to your Downloads folder.
  • Settings: your format and audio preferences, plus an anonymous device ID and Pro status, are stored locally.

What We DON'T Collect

  • We do NOT upload your screenshots, recordings, or PDFs anywhere.
  • We do NOT collect your browsing history, page contents, name, email, or location.
  • We do NOT run analytics, tracking, or advertising, and we never sell data.

Feedback

If you choose to use the in-extension Send feedback form, the message you type is sent to our server so we can read it and improve the product. An email address is sent only if you choose to add one (so we can reply). This is entirely optional and is only sent when you click Send. We do not collect any feedback automatically.

The Only Network Connection

NeuraShot's single network call is a lightweight Pro licence check to our secure proxy, sending only your anonymous device ID or NB-XXXX-XXXX code. Recordings are saved as WebM and PDFs are generated on-device with a bundled library — no cloud services are used for capture.

Permissions

  • activeTab / tabs / host access: to capture the page (and, for All Tabs, other tabs in the window) you choose.
  • downloads: to save captures to your Downloads folder.
  • offscreen: to run screen recording and PDF generation.
  • storage: to save preferences and Pro status locally.

Payments & Contact

Pro payments are handled by Stripe; the extension never sees your card details. Uninstalling removes all local data. Questions: info@neurabolt.ai. Complies with the Chrome Web Store policies, GDPR, and CCPA where applicable.
